About us


Tradition since 1889

The Catering was founded in 1889 by Mr. Dean Smith first in the city of Omaha, Nebraska. Since then, The Catering has grown to a chain of 58 restaurants spread across the United States and Canada. We are commited to cook food only using natural, biological, ecological and locally sourced ingredients to provide you the best experience in gourmet food in North America. See our locations here !

Important notice : Please note that pricing on our meals may be impacted and inconsistent due to shortages caused by recent events. In case you're interested, please check on the menu of the specific restaurant you would like to dine in. Thank you for your understanding and we apologize for the inconvenience.
